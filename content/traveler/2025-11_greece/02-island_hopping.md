<h4 class="article-subheader">Your Ferry Will Depart From Gate E9</h4>
"Ok great! And where is E9?"

"That way," the Blue Star Ferries ticket agent points with the enthusiasm of
of a student twirling her hair with a pencil in an 80s movie.

"THAT WAY!!!" I shout to our family gathered at the crosswalk 200 yards away.
Unfortunately I am at the obtuse angle of the triangle formed between them, me
and "that way".

"I still need to see 3 more passports," the gate agent smarmily remarks.

*ohh for fucks sake* I mutter as I sprint towards our gaggle of familial
waddlers.

Three minutes later I return to the kiosk with three passports sheathed in their
own uniquely frustrating cloaks.

*"Here"* I mutter between my labored breathing as I slam the passports down on
the metal countertop.

"I'm sorry, we are too close to the ferry departure time to book you from this
office. You must book your ticket at the desk next to the departure terminal."

"And thats..."

"That way"
